ABOUT

Real people, real businesses, real moments — made cinematic.

MR ARTS is the newest company under MK Productions: the commercial creative media side of the brand, built to help local businesses, creators, events and artists show themselves properly.

"Business content shouldn't feel like an ad. It should feel real, emotional, and worth watching." — MR ARTS brand philosophy
Parent Brand

MR ARTS operates under MK Productions.

MK Productions is the parent studio — home to narrative short films and long-term creative projects. MR ARTS is its own distinct, client-facing brand: the side built specifically for businesses, brands, events and creators who need professional content, not a side project.

Founders

Two disciplines, one output.

Every MR ARTS project runs through both a creative and a technical lens before it's called finished.

FOUNDER — 01

Mathesh Kishore

Technical Director · Cinematographer · Editor · Director

Mathesh handles the technical and visual side of MR ARTS — cinematography, camera operation, editing, colour grading, VFX, sound design and direction.

His focus is making every frame look premium, emotional and intentional, from the first shot to final delivery.

FOUNDER — 02

Rahul Nair

Creative Director · Content Strategist

Rahul leads creative planning, writing, direction and content strategy — shaping what a video should say and how the story should flow.

He also drives ideas, scripts, captions and the overall brand direction across every platform MR ARTS shows up on.

How We Work

Strategy and execution, in the same room.

Rahul — story, strategy, direction

Mathesh — visuals, technical execution, delivery
