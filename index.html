Who We Are

Two founders. One creative partnership.

MR ARTS is the commercial creative media brand under MK Productions, co-owned by Mathesh Kishore and Rahul Nair. Rahul leads creative direction and content strategy — the story, the script, the plan. Mathesh leads the technical and visual execution — camera, edit, colour, and final delivery.

Together, that means every project is both cinematic and considered — content that feels real, not just polished.
